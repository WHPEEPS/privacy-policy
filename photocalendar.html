📅 PhotoCalendar

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Calendar events entered by the user (title, date, memo)
  • Photos selected from gallery or camera (stored on device only)
  • Subscription status (expiry date stored encrypted on device)
  • App settings (theme, language preferences)

2. How We Store Information

All data is stored only on your device. No personal data is transmitted to external servers. When sharing events via .dat files, only the events you choose to share are included in the file.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization.
  • Google Play / App Store: Handles subscription payments.

4. Permissions Used

  • Camera: To take photos for monthly calendar
  • Photo Library: To select photos from gallery
  • Notifications: To send event and D-Day reminders
  • Storage: To save calendar PDF files

5. Data Deletion

All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. GDPR (EU Users)

Users in the European Union have the right to access, correct, and delete their personal data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data by uninstalling the app. For ad personalization opt-out, please visit Google's privacy settings.
