# Privacy Policy - WHPEEPS Apps

Last updated: March 15, 2026

---

## PlantCare AI

### Camera Permission
PlantCare AI uses camera permission solely to take photos of plants for disease diagnosis.

### Data Collection
- Photos are processed on-device only
- No photos are sent to external servers
- No personal information is collected

---

## BabyCry AI

### Microphone Permission
BabyCry AI uses microphone permission solely to record and analyze baby cry sounds.

### Data Collection
- Audio recordings are processed on-device only
- No audio data is sent to external servers
- No personal information is collected
